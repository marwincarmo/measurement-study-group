**Measuring Psychology** is a global community and study group of aspiring and current students, professors, and interested people that help each other. We all have an interest in measurement theory for psychology and psychometrics. Through *discussions*, *solidarity*, and *openness*, we are boosting psychological science by improving measurement.

<h1>Community</h1>
</div>

As you can see on the map, we have a growing community from almost all parts of the world, with different backgrounds. For instance, we are: 

  a. both from academia and industry;  
  b. from undergraduate students to Ph.D.’s;  
  c. from psychology, education, statistics, mathematics, and many more.
  
We all have an important interest, to improve our area of research by learning and applying measurement theory and psychometrics in our studies. Most importantly, we are an open-arms community, built to have a safe environment to study together, exchange ideas, and collaborate. Please, read the [About](about.html) page to see our code of conduct.
